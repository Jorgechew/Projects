James Hetfield

Lead Singer


James Hetfield co-founded Metallica and is the lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist.

He's a talented songwriter, contributing to many of Metallica's iconic hits.

Hetfield struggled with addiction and entered rehab in 2001, leading to his recovery and renewed focus on music.

Beyond music, he's interested in hot rodding and custom car building, even founding a car show.


Kirk Hammett

Guitar Machine


Kirk Hammett is Metallica's lead guitarist, known for his melodic and expressive solos.

He replaced guitarist Dave Mustaine in 1983, shortly after the band's formation.

Hammett is a horror movie enthusiast and has his own collection of horror memorabilia.

He's of Filipino and Irish descent, adding to Metallica's diverse cultural mix.


Lars Ulrich

Drummer


Lars Ulrich co-founded Metallica in 1981.

He's Danish and passionate about art collecting.

Known for the Napster lawsuit in 2000s.

Maintains a fit lifestyle and loves cycling.


Robert Trujillo

Bass Player


Robert Trujillo joined Metallica as a bassist in 2003.

He was previously a member of bands like Suicidal Tendencies and Ozzy Osbourne's band.

Trujillo is known for his distinctive bass playing style and energetic stage presence.

He's of Mexican and Native American descent, contributing to Metallica's diverse lineup.
